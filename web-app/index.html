How to Play

  • Two chefs take turns packing pieces into the box.
  • Pieces only cover empty cells — never food or garnish.
  • Fill a 3×3 compartment to seal it: the chef with more food inside earns +5 points.
  • Golden cells award +3 to whoever covers them. Wasabi burns −3 — sometimes worth it!
  • Cover the matcha to take an extra turn at once. Omakase mode (in settings) hides all specials until they are covered!
  • The compartment marked with a star is the VIP order — own it when sealed for +10 instead of +5. And whoever packs the very last piece slams the lid for +2!
  • Fair starts: the first box's starter is random, then the loser of each box packs first in the next one (draws alternate).
  • When nothing fits anywhere, the box is sealed. Most points (cells + bonuses) wins.
  • Mouse only: click a piece (or right-click anywhere on the box to rotate it), then click a cell to pack it.
  • Keyboard only: 1–7 pick a piece (focus jumps back to the box), arrow keys move, Enter packs, R rotates.
  • Chef 2 can be a friend or a computer chef — pick under their name. Raise the difficulty in settings with more garnish.
  • Turn on Endless rush in settings to race a countdown: pack as many boxes as you can before time runs out — each full box is banked and a fresh one is dealt, and the higher running total wins when the clock hits zero.
