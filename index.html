How to play

  • Click a highlighted cell to place your token.
  • Any of your opponent's tokens caught in a straight line between your new token and an existing one are flipped.
  • If you cannot move, your turn is skipped.
  • The game ends when neither player can move. Most tokens wins!

Keyboard

  • Arrow keys – move around the board
  • Enter / Space – place token
  • N – new game
  • H – toggle hints

🌧️ Why can I only jump on certain squares?

You must sandwich your opponent's tokens

A square is only available to play if your new token creates a straight line — horizontal, vertical, or diagonal — where one or more of your opponent's tokens sit between your new token and one of your existing tokens. Every trapped token then flips to your colour.

If no such line exists from a square, that square is blocked. That is why you cannot click just anywhere!

No sandwich = blocked square

If placing on a square would not trap any opponent token — because there is no friendly token completing the line, or the line ends at an empty cell or the board edge — that square is not a legal move and stays unclickable.
